CreatureHistoryEvent::GetMoniker1 (  ) 

Accessor method for moniker 1 Moniker 1 is the first moniker associated with this event. In conception and splicing, it is one of the conceiving creatures. In cloning, it is the cloned creature. (both ways) In laying an egg, it is the moniker of the egg laid. In becoming pregnant, it's the creature that made this one pregnant In making another pregnant, it's the pregnant creature. In a child being born, it's the other parent of the child

Returns:
The first moniker associated with this event
